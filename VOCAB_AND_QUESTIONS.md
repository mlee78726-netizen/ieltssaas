## 1. Thema: Hobbies & Free Time

### Fragen (Part 1 Style)
1. What do you like to do in your free time?
2. Did you have any hobbies when you were a child?
3. Is it important to have a hobby? Why?
4. What is a new hobby you would like to try in the future?

### Ziel-Wortschatz (High-Level & Natural)
- **Passionate about**: "I'm quite passionate about..." (statt "I like")
- **Indulge in**: "Whenever I have a moment, I like to indulge in some light reading."
- **Take up**: "I'm actually planning to take up photography next year."
- **Unwind / Decompress**: "It's the perfect way for me to unwind after a long day."
- **Recharge my batteries**: "It really helps me recharge my batteries for the upcoming week."
- **A sense of accomplishment**: "Completing a piece of art gives me a real sense of accomplishment."
- **Keeps me sharp**: "I enjoy chess because it really keeps me sharp."

## 2. Thema: Home & Accommodation

### Fragen (Part 1 Style)
1. Do you live in a house or an apartment?
2. Which is your favorite room in your home? Why?
3. How long have you lived there?
4. What would you like to change about your home?

### Ziel-Wortschatz (High-Level & Natural)
- **Spacious / Airy**: "The living room is quite spacious and airy."
- **Centrally located**: "My apartment is centrally located, which is very convenient."
- **Within walking distance**: "It's great being within walking distance of the local shops."
- **Minimalist decor**: "I've gone for a fairly minimalist decor to keep things simple."
- **State-of-the-art**: "The kitchen is equipped with state-of-the-art appliances."
- **Tucked away**: "It's a quiet neighborhood, tucked away from the main road."
- **Make some improvements**: "I've been meaning to make some improvements to the balcony."

## 3. Thema: Work & Studies

### Fragen (Part 1 Style)
1. Do you work or are you a student?
2. What exactly do you do? / What are you studying?
3. Why did you choose that job/major?
4. What is the most interesting part of your job/studies?

### Ziel-Wortschatz (High-Level & Natural)
- **Rewarding / Fulfilling**: "I find my current role incredibly rewarding."
- **Challenging but doable**: "The coursework is challenging but doable if you stay organized."
- **Career prospects**: "I chose this major because of the excellent career prospects."
- **Broaden my horizons**: "Studying this has really helped me broaden my horizons."
- **Hands-on experience**: "The best part is getting some real hands-on experience in the field."
- **Collaborative environment**: "We work in a very collaborative environment."
- **Hectic / Fast-paced**: "The atmosphere at work is usually quite hectic but exciting."
